Welcome to Volney Creek

Located at the foot of the majestic Beartooth Mountains in the heart of Montana's Big Sky Country, is Volney Creek Ranch, a 1300- acre piece of paradise. This pristine setting is the home of our Volney Creek Arabians. Raising our horses on pure mountain water and the finest Montana timothy & alfalfa grasses, we breed our small band of champion brood mares to only the finest stallions from around the world to produce champion quality foals.

While we are relatively new to the Arabian breeding business, our mission is timeless: to produce the classic Arabian 'type' foal displaying not only refined conformation but also the unique athleticism for which the Arabian horse is so well known.

If you love Arabian horses and have always dreamed of owning them, then Volney Creek Arabians is the perfect place to learn about making that dream a reality.

We invite you to share our dream and visit us in this very special part of the world. At Volney Creek Arabians we are committed to providing you with the Arabian horse of your dreams. Come visit us and see how Arabians thrive under the Montana Big Sky.
